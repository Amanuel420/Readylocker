1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Identification Information

  • Name, Email, Phone, Address
  • User account credentials
  • Payment information (processed through secure third parties)

1.2 Locker Usage Data

  • Access logs (date, time, duration)
  • Locker numbers and access credentials used
  • Package delivery and retrieval timestamps
  • Package dimensions and carrier info

1.3 Device and Technical Information

We collect IP addresses, device types, operating systems, and location data (if enabled) to improve system performance.

2. HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATION

  • Directly: When you register, use lockers, or contact support.
  • Automatically: Via cookies on our site or access logs on our lockers.
  • Third Parties: From property managers, delivery carriers, and payment processors.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To operate locker systems and process deliveries.
  • To send notifications (access codes, delivery alerts).
  • To verify identity and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations and property rules.

4. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with:

  • Property Managers: To manage resident compliance and property operations.
  • Service Providers: For cloud storage, payment processing, and SMS delivery.
  • Delivery Carriers: Limited data to facilitate drop-offs.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or subpoena.

5. VIDEO SURVEILLANCE AND SECURITY CAMERAS

Important Notice

Some Ready Lockers installations include security cameras. Appropriate signage will be posted where applicable.

Footage is used to deter theft and investigate incidents. Routine footage is retained for 30-90 days unless flagged for an investigation.

6. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use essential, performance, and functional cookies. You can control cookie settings via your browser. Blocking essential cookies may affect functionality.

7. DATA RETENTION

  • Account Data: Retained while active. Deleted after 2 years of inactivity.
  • Locker Logs: Retained for 2 years.
  • Video: 30-90 days.

8. DATA SECURITY

We use encryption (TLS/SSL), secure servers, and access controls. While we strive for security, no system is absolute; you are responsible for keeping your credentials safe.

9. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, or opt-out of marketing communications. Contact privacy@readylockers.com to exercise these rights.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are for users 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS (CCPA)

California residents have rights to know what data is collected and request deletion. We do not sell personal information. Requests can be made via email or phone.

12. EUROPEAN USERS (GDPR)

EEA/UK users have rights to access, rectification, erasure, and portability. We process data based on contract performance, consent, and legitimate interests.

13. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Ready Lockers is US-based. Data is transferred to the US using standard contractual clauses and appropriate safeguards.

14. THIRD-PARTY LINKS AND SERVICES

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites linked in our services.
